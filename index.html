Vietnam War

The Vietnam War is one of the most hard fought wars in U.S. history. It lasted 19 years, and millions of lives were lost.
If you didn't know, the Vietnam War, was a war against North Vietnam, and South Vietnam. North Vietnam was communist, and they wanted South Vientnam to be communist too. So they started invading South Vietnam to make them communist, like them.

The Struggles and Dangers

The struggles of the Vietnam War were real, American and South Vietnam soliders were marching through the dense fog and rain of the Vietnam forests. They had to constantly swat away mosquitos while they were either shooting, or living as normally as a person in war could. Not only did these little pests bother soliders non-stop, they were forced to deal with the environment as well. The forests were dense, and you could usually see very little. The forest floor muddy and slippy. All while having to be on high alert, just in case in an enemey was nearby.

Many people who leave with the burdon of thier families, are weighed with the thought that they might never make it back to them. And they might die alone without them.
